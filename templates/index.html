DJam: Easy Music

About DJam

What is DJam?

Use DJam to easily generate playlists from your favorite artists! Whether you want to listen to music by yourself or with others, DJam makes it easy to generate a playlist that has songs from artists that you know you already like.

DJam was developed in May 2018 by Navina Mathew, Priyank Varshney, and Zoie MacDougall.


How does DJam work?

To use DJam, just enter the artists you like and, if you want, specify how much you like each artist—it's that simple! Once you've entered the artists you'd like, DJam uses the music API last.fm to find popular songs from each artist you'd like to listen to.

Generate a Playlist

To generate a playlist, enter the names of ten artists and indicate how much you like each artist. When you're done, just click "Submit" and DJam will display your generated playlist.

Note: Give 10's to your favorite artists, and give 1's to your least favorite artists.
